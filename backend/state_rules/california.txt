California Driving Rules and Regulations

SPEED LIMITS:
- Residential areas: 25 mph
- Business districts: 25 mph
- School zones: 25 mph (when children present)
- Highways: 65 mph (unless posted otherwise)
- Freeways: 70 mph (in rural areas)

RIGHT OF WAY:
- Yield to pedestrians in crosswalks
- Yield to vehicles already in intersection
- At four-way stops: first to arrive has right of way

PARKING RULES:
- No parking within 15 feet of fire hydrant
- No parking in red zones
- Parallel parking must be within 18 inches of curb

SPECIAL CALIFORNIA LAWS:
- Hands-free phone use required while driving
- Move Over Law: move over for emergency vehicles
- Zipper merge required in construction zones
